Understanding Glaucoma

Glaucoma is a major public health concern and one of the leading causes of irreversible vision loss worldwide. As of 2020, around 3.61 million people are blind and 4.14 million are visually impaired due to this condition. Early detection and regular intraocular pressure (IOP) monitoring are crucial in managing this disease effectively.

Non-Contact IOP Monitoring

Our project introduces a portable device for home-based intraocular pressure measurement without direct eye contact.

Advanced Sensor Integration

Replacing traditional IR sensors with a Passive Infrared (PIR) sensor enhances the accuracy of pressure detection.

Cloud-Enabled Smart Device

Powered by Arduino Nano and BMP180, our system stores patient data on the cloud via Wi-Fi, enabling continuous tracking.
