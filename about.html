Glaucoma Care

Early detection and pressure monitoring to prevent irreversible vision loss.

Our team uses state-of-the-art non-contact tonometers and cloud-based data tracking to monitor intraocular pressure (IOP) in patients at risk of or living with glaucoma.

Vision Screening

Routine eye checks for early diagnosis of refractive errors.

Our screening department performs detailed vision analysis to detect nearsightedness, farsightedness, and other common eye issues across all age groups.

Optometry

Specialized prescription and correction solutions for visual comfort.

Our optometry services include accurate eye tests, lens prescriptions, and consultations for glasses and contact lenses with advanced technology.

Ophthalmic Surgery

Advanced surgical care for complex eye conditions.

We offer cutting-edge surgical interventions for cataracts, glaucoma, retinal disorders, and more under the supervision of skilled ophthalmic surgeons.

Child Eye Care

Gentle and effective care for children's eye health.

Our pediatric eye specialists provide child-friendly diagnostics, therapy for lazy eye, vision correction, and preventive care in a comfortable setting.
